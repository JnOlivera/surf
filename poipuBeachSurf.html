Additional information

If you are doing a surf lesson for the first time or even a few times, it’s helpful to check out this ‘how to stand up on the board when catching a wave’ video. It’s called the pop up technique.

(90 minutes including land lesson, water safety, and water time) Poipu Beach Surf Lessons for Beginners offers an exciting and safe introduction to the exhilarating world of surfing. Located in the picturesque sunny south shore, our experienced instructors provide personalized lessons tailored to beginners. Whether you’re a first-time surfer or looking to improve your skills, our patient and knowledgeable guides will teach you the basics of paddling, balancing, and riding waves. With a focus on building confidence, our lessons guarantee an enjoyable learning experience amidst the stunning backdrop of the beautiful blue ocean and beaches.

~Group surf lesson 2-8 shared lessons with other parties (10 + ages) This is a shared experience, if you don’t mind learning to surf with other people in a group setting. We always have a minimum of 1 instructor for 4 people and 2 instructors 5-8 people.

~ Ohana (family) surf lesson (private group) 3 to 8 people (10+ages) A family that surfs together stays together! This experience is our most popular perfect for couples, small groups, and the whole family to enjoy the ocean and riding waves together! IF you have children younger than 10 we can take them out with an additional instructor in the Kids Private Surf Lesson at the same time. 1 instructor for 4 people and 2 instructors 5-8 people.

~ Kids Private Surf Lessons (12 and under) We love sharing with the keiki! We give special attention focusing on the safety, fun, and sensitivity of young children)1 to 2 is 1 instructor, 3 or more 2 instructors for safety and attention to detail for their best experience, especially first time surfing and being in the ocean. 1 instructor for 4 people and 2 instructors 5-8 people.

~ Private surf lessons (one on one with a surf instructor) One on one guidance with our skilled instructors gives you personalized attention whether you are a beginner, learning to advance to the next level of catching your own waves or an intermediate surfer wanting to refine and grow your surfing skills and ability to read waves and learn a new surf break.
